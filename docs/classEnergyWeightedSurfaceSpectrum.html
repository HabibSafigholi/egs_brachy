Detailed Description

A class for scoring the energy weighted spectrum (normalized to total radiant energy) of particles on the surface of a source geometry.

See Taylor & Rogers, Med. Phys., 35 , 4933 – 4944, 2008

Sample input:

:start spectrum scoring:
    type = energy weighted surface
    particle type = photon
    minimum energy = 0.001
    maximum energy = 1.00
    number of bins = 1000
    output format = xmgr
:stop spectrum scoring:
